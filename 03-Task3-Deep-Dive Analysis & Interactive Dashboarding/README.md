Task 3: Deep-Dive Analysis & Interactive Dashboard
📌 Objective

The objective of Task 3 is to perform a focused deep-dive business analysis and build an interactive dashboard that highlights key performance indicators (KPIs) and enables dynamic exploration of business insights from the Online Retail dataset.

This task emphasizes business thinking, KPI design, and storytelling through dashboards.

📊 Dataset

Source: Online Retail Dataset from kaggle
Data Used: Cleaned dataset from Task 1 & Task 2

🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

- Python – Data preparation

- SQL – KPI validation

- Power BI – Interactive dashboard development
- 

🎯 Core KPIs Defined

- Total Revenue

- Total Transactions

- Average Order Value (AOV)

- Total Customers


🔎 Deep-Dive Analysis Focus

- Customer & Product Performance Analysis, including:

- Monthly revenue trends

- Top revenue-generating products

- Customer purchase frequency

- Revenue distribution by country

- Customer segmentation (New vs Returning)
- 

📈 Interactive Dashboard (Power BI)

The dashboard allows users to explore performance through:

Key Visuals

Monthly Revenue Trend

Top 5 Revenue-Generating Products

Top Customers by Purchase Frequency

Customer Segmentation

Revenue Distribution by Country

Filters Available

Country

Month / Year


💡 Key Insights

Revenue peaked in Q4 2011, showing strong seasonal demand

A small group of products drives a large share of revenue

The UK dominates total revenue contribution

Returning customers contribute significantly to overall sales
