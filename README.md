## 📌 Task Descriptions

### ✅ **Task 1 – Data Cleaning & Wrangling**
- Load raw Online Retail dataset.
- Cleaned data by handling missing values, negative quantities, duplicates.
- Created a derived revenue column:  
  **TotalAmount = Quantity × UnitPrice**

---

### 📊 **Task 2 – Exploratory Data Analysis & Business Intelligence**
- Performed exploratory analysis to understand sales distribution.
- Validated key business metrics using SQL.
- Built EDA visuals and a Power BI dashboard covering:
  - Revenue trends
  - Top products
  - Country-wise revenue

🔗 SQL queries are available here:  
`sql/sql_queries.sql`

---

### 📈 **Task 3 – Interactive Dashboard**
- Defined and visualized core KPIs:
  - Total Revenue
  - Total Transactions
  - Average Order Value
  - Total Customers
- Built interactive visuals:
  - Monthly revenue trend
  - Top performing products
  - Customer behavior analysis
  - Revenue by country
  - Filters for month, country, product, Year

Screenshot preview:  
`visuals/dashboard_screenshot.png`

---

### 📖 **Task 4 – Data Storytelling & Validation**
- Consolidated insights into a coherent business narrative.
- Created slides explaining trends and business implications.
- Validated hypothesis on customer behavior.
- Demonstrated actionable insights from dashboard findings.

Slides and hypothesis summary are included under:
`04-Task-4-Data-Storytelling/`

---
📌 Key Business Insights

- **Revenue shows increasing trends** through 2011, with seasonal peaks.
- **United Kingdom contributes the highest revenue** compared to other countries.
- A **small number of products generate most revenue**, indicating concentrated performance.
- **New customers make up the majority**, but there is a strong base of returning customers — suggesting room for improved retention.
